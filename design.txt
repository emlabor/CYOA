Design of the CYOA game:
	HEALTH: +medic, -wounds
	STRENGTH: +food, +rest, -hunger, -exhaustion

	STORY: The power is out. You are tyring to get home in New York City.

		The time it 20:00. You have four hours. Some decisions will cost more time than others.

		FIRST DECISION: You need a source of light. Your phone is at 15%.
			1. Use your phone flashlight (20:00)
				a. Losing power
			2. Buy a real flashlight (20:15)
				a. Losing power
				b. Bad reputation
				

		SECOND DECISION: Now that you have light, time to start heading home.
			1. West
			2. North
